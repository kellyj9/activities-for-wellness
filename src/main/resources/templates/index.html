What is Wellness?

Wellness can be envisioned as:

"...not the absence of disease, illness, and stress, but as the presence of a positive purpose in life, satisfying work and play, joyful relationships, a healthy body and living environment, and happiness..."
(Dunn, 1961, as cited by the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration [SAMHSA], 2010).

What are the Dimensions of Wellness?

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA, 2017) identifies eight dimensions of wellness to focus on to optimize health.

The dimensions of wellness include:

  • Emotional
  • Spiritual
  • Intellectual
  • Physical
  • Environmental
  • Financial
  • Occupational
  • Social

In which of the dimensions of wellness do you feel strongest? What dimensions would you like to work on?
