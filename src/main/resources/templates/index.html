Optimize your wellness through a holistic approach to health.

This website presents eight dimension of wellness and provides a way for you to create activity lists to improve your health and wellbeing in each dimension.

What is Wellness?

Wellness can be envisioned as:

"...not the absence of disease, illness, and stress, but as the presence of a positive purpose in life, satisfying work and play, joyful relationships, a healthy body and living environment, and happiness..."
(Dunn, 1961, as cited by the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration [SAMHSA], 2010).

What are the Dimensions of Wellness?

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration identifies eight basic dimensions of wellness to focus on to optimize health (SAMHSA, 2017).

The dimensions of wellness include:
  • Emotional
  • Spiritual
  • Intellectual
  • Physical
  • Environmental
  • Financial
  • Occupational
  • Social

Through our activities, we can address our needs in each dimension. Our activities can be planned according to factors such as our goals, beliefs, values, culture, personality, and life experiences.
