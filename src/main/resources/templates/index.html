What is Wellness?

"Wellness can be envisioned as not the absence of disease, illness, and stress, but as the presence of a positive purpose in life, satisfying work and play, joyful relationships, a healthy body and living environment, and happiness." [https://mfpcc.samhsa.gov/ENewsArticles/Article12b_2017.aspx, last retreived 11/26/2021]

What are the Dimensions of Wellness?

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) has identified eight dimensions of wellness to focus on to optimize health. The eight dimensions include: emotional, spiritual, intellectual, physical, environmental, financial, occupational, and social.
