What is Wellness?

Wellness is more than a state of feeling well. Wellness is actively living a lifestyle that leads to optimal health and wellbeing. You can optimize your wellness despite the presence of illness or stress. With the concept of wellness, a person is seen from different dimensions to create a holistic perspective.

Focusing too much or too little in one dimension can cause us to feel imbalanced. Our activities in one dimension can influence our wellness in other dimensions. In order to optimize our wellness, we pursue activities in all dimensions of wellness.

Our activities for wellness can be planned according to factors such as our goals, beliefs, values, culture, personality, and life experiences.
