What is Wellness?

Wellness is more than a state of feeling well. Wellness is actively living a lifestyle that leads to optimal health and wellbeing. You can improve and maintain your wellness despite the presence of illness or stress. With the concept of wellness, a person is seen from different dimensions to create a holistic perspective.

Find a personal harmony with your activities.

All dimensions of wellness need our attention. Our activities in one dimension can influence our wellness in other dimensions. If we only pay attention to some of the dimensions, our wellness in the other dimensions will be affected.

However, there doesn't need to be a perfect balance among all the dimensions. Activities can be planned according to factors such as our goals, beliefs, values, culture, personality, and life experiences. The goal is to find a personal harmony with the dimensions that is most authentic for you.
